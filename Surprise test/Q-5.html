5)What are the HTML entities explain examples of two entities.

->An HTML entity is a piece of text that begins with an ampersand(&) and ends at semicolon(;).

->They are used to display the reserved characters and invisible characters.

->For ex.

->(<)less than:here we use, "<"

->(>)greater than:here we use, ">"

->(space)here we use, " "

->(")for quotation we use, (")
